Welcome to the Job Finder

First: Click “Continue” to choose your Level of Education.

Secondly: Choose the amount of training desired (“no training” = <6 weeks; “training required” = 6 weeks–5 years or a bachelor’s; “extensive training” = >5 years or requires a master’s+).

Thirdly: Pick the job category in the chosen Level of Education.

Fourthly: View the job names in that category, their mean annual wage before taxes, and affordability index (annual wage/monthly rent).

Fifth: Download the spreadsheet with job name, #jobs available, mean annual wage before taxes, and affordability index.
